Description

This map displays the birthplaces and where women scientists from the 20th century worked while they were innovating in the field of radiology

Why We Chose This

The innovations and work done by these important women have heavily influenced and impacted the field of medical science. Our goal for this mapping project is to provide information to the public in a concise and appealing format
